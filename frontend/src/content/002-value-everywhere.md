Over the last few years, I started noticing a frustrating pattern: our most valuable context was constantly getting trapped in isolated silos. Between chat threads, Google Drive, AWS buckets, Jira tickets, and Confluence pages, I was spending an exhausting amount of time trying to sift through the noise. 

I started a long-term project to gain back the knowledge buried in those disparate documents and put them to work for our team. The first step was combine the data sources and convert them into an understood format that could easily be developed with and understood. 

```demo:multisource
```

Click between the source tabs. The left pane is what the content looks like in its native tool; the right pane is the markdown rendition that downstream pipelines consume.
